  # Depth in the Age of AI

  A short field guide to working with AI without losing depth.

  [Read the guide →](./index.html)

  ## What's inside

  - **A case study** — a real bug, three "fixes," and the cost of stopping too soon
  - **Why depth still matters** — what production-got-cheap actually means for engineers
  - **Six tactics for catching the slip** — how to verify AI output
  - **What to invest in** — the skills that compound, the ones that don't
  - **Roadmap to mastery** — ten topics worth going deep on, with sources and strategies
  - **Where to practice** — curated hands-on resources

  ## Background

  Field notes from a debugging session in early 2026 where an AI's first two
  "fixes" looked correct, passed tests, and would have shipped a partial fix
  without an engineer with the depth to push back. The rest of the guide came
  out of the conversation that followed.
